May 2017 - July 2017

_PROJECT TYPE:

Industrial

_MENTOR:

Ekta Rohra Jafri, Design Director - Group Manager at IBM Interactive Experience

_ROLE:

Solo Project: User Research, Interaction Design, Visual Design

_ABOUT THE PROJECT:

In the summer of 2017, I worked at an NCR based startup named Quizential, which is a knowledge networking platform.

For three months, I worked in startup mode to understand the root cause behind the low usage numbers of the platform. I was also responsible for conceptualizing and designing implementable solutions to the discovered issues. I have skipped details as the project is covered under an NDA, but I would love to share the process.

I worked closely with my fellow intern Robin Goyal, and revamped the overall UX of the website. We also conceptualized the website's Find-A-Mentor feature.
