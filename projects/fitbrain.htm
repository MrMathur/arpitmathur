_ABOUT THE PROJECT:

I am currently a Human-Centered Engineer at the MITRE Corporation in the Collaboration and Social Computing Group. I completed my Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon in 2014.

I am interested in the intersection of technology and people, looking to make seamless, frictionless interactions. My passion is researching and designing interactions for Human - Robot Interactions. I work towards creating human-robot interactions even better than human-human interactions.
