_PROJECT TYPE:

_GUIDE:

Professor Pradeep Yammiyavar

_ROLE:

Solo Project: Interaction Design, Visual Design

_ABOUT THE PROJECT:

This project was done as part of the Winter Internship at UE-HCI Lab in IIT Guwahati, in December 2017.

I worked on identifying problems behind the non-adoption of electric vehicles as a mode of transport in India. Further I identified solutions that could be delivered through a mobile application, that helped in battery management and maintenance.

Other interns at the lab had been working on different solutions to make e-vehicles viable in India, in the domain of Industrial Design, Environmental Design and Graphic Design. I worked alone on this mobile based solution in the field of Interaction Design.
