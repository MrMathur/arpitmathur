FITBrain: An Instructional Design approach to Alzheimer's Disease

_LOCATION:

Department of Design, IIT Guwahati

_DURATION:

January 2018 - May 2018

_PROJECT TYPE:

Academic

_GUIDE:

Assistant Professor Abhishek Srivastava

_ROLE:

Group Project: User Research, Instructional Design, Interaction Design

_ABOUT THE PROJECT:

This project was done as part of our Instructional Design elective. The goal of the project was to use instructional design methodologies to solve user needs.

We decided to work for Alzheimer's disease patients on the suggestion of our course instructor. The goal of this project was to use the tools provided by the ADDIE design methodlogy to understand the patients, and give them instruction to solve their needs.

I did this project with my classmates, Nilotpal Nayan, Mukesh Kumar Ranjan, and Abhishek Narayan Singh. I was heavily involved in each stage of research, conceptualization, and prototyping.
