NEX: IIT Guwahati's Social Time Mangement Application

_LOCATION:

IIT Guwahati

_DURATION:

Jan 2017 - Feb 2017

_PROJECT TYPE:

Side Project

_MENTOR:

Coding Club, IIT Guwahati

_ROLE:

Solo Project: Visual Design

_ABOUT THE PROJECT:

As part of the Coding Club in 2017, I attempted to make a social networking cum time management application, to be used by students across the institute's intranet.

It was a good exercise in visual design, as I attempted to make a beautiful interface, without compromising the usability of the system. I did not have to focus my attention on the working of the network, just the front-end was my responsibility.

We were unable to obtain permission from the institute to launch the web platform, however it was the first project that introduced my to the art of web design, and I have been hooked ever since.
